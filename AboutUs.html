Welcome to ZooParc Zoological Park

ZooParc Zoological Park, tucked away in 70 hectares of everdant surroundings, is a wildlife enthusiast'sand adventurer's dream come true.

Encountering the wonders of the animal realm is an engaging experienceavailable at our park, which is home to over 2,000 animals representing200 different species.

Each step you take in our park brings you
closer to nature, from the
captivating giant pandasandstately Asian elephants to the colorful poison frogs andbreathtaking bald eagles.

At ZooParc, we're not simply a zoo; we're a community committed to educating people andprotecting wildlife.

Our goal is toinspire the next generation of wildlife warriors whilesafeguarding endangered species and theirecosystems.

One visit at a time, we are dedicated to
changing the world with the help of our
enthusiastic teamof employees,volunteers, andcommunity members.
